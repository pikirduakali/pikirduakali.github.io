01About Me

I'm a craftsman who sometimes takes
things too seriously.

Barista by trade, designer by stubbornness. I've spent years brewing coffee and creating all sorta things that range from genuinely useful to "we thought this was a good idea."

I care about craft — not in a precious way, but in the "let's make this actually work" way. The function matters. The result matters more.
