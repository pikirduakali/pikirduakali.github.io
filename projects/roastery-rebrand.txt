Roastery Rebrand
Branding | 2025 | Coffee Craft Co.

A full brand identity overhaul for a local specialty coffee roaster that wanted to move from generic to distinctive. The brief was simple — create a system that feels handcrafted but scalable, warm but modern.

The logomark was built around a stylised coffee cherry that doubles as a rising sun — referencing early mornings and the roast cycle. The geometry is tight, but the curves keep it approachable.

Colour palette was anchored by a deep rust (their signature roast level) with warm ivory and slate grey supporting. The type pair — a grotesque for headlines and a humanist sans for body — mirrors that same contrast.

Deliverables included a full stationery suite, packaging roll-out across four single-origin SKUs, digital brand guidelines, and signage for their flagship brew bar.

The rebrand launched in early 2025 and the new look has been rolled out across their cafés in Jakarta and Bandung.
